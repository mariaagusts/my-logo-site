Pricing.

Indicative engagement tiers · ISK with EUR equivalents · 2026

Most engagements are quoted as a fixed fee for a defined scope. Indicative ranges below; the right number depends on complexity, deadline, and whether the work is bilingual.

First call is free. We agree the scope and a fixed fee in writing before any work begins. Out-of-scope requests are estimated separately. Travel outside Reykjavík is charged at cost.

Scope & change

Scope is fixed in writing. Anything outside it is quoted separately before work begins, never billed as a surprise.

Invoicing

50% on signature, 50% on delivery for fixed-fee engagements. Retained engagements are invoiced monthly in advance.

Currency

ISK is the contracted currency. EUR equivalents are indicative and may be invoiced on request at the day's reference rate.

VAT

Prices are exclusive of 24% Icelandic VAT where applicable. Foreign clients: VAT handled per applicable rules.
