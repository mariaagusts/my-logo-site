About me

I help small businesses turn messy operations into clear, repeatable ways of working. The output is practical: simpler workflows, clearer ownership, and simple routines that make work move.

My background spans finance, operations, and business development, including senior roles in regulated environments. It’s work I enjoy: finding the bottlenecks, clarifying responsibilities, and building a simple structure that people actually use.

I’m used to working in regulated environments where accuracy, documentation, and follow-through matter.

When needed, I also translate and edit Icelandic and English business writing when tone and accuracy matter, from web content to internal documentation and reports. The goal is English that reads naturally while staying faithful to the Icelandic meaning.

Based in Iceland, working with clients locally and internationally. I take on short, focused engagements or ongoing support.

Email hello@multabene.is with what’s not working today, what better looks like, and your timeline.
