About me

I help small businesses untangle messy workflows and turn decisions into follow-through. The output is practical: a clearer process, templates people actually use, and documentation that makes day to day work easier.

I also translate and edit Icelandic and English business writing, especially where tone and accuracy matter: web content, client communication, internal documentation, and professional reports. I aim for English that reads naturally while staying faithful to the Icelandic meaning.

I studied Economics at Harvard and Management Research at Oxford. I’ve worked across finance, operations, and business development, including senior roles in regulated environments. Outside of work, life revolves around my two daughters and the small moments that keep everything grounded.

Based in Iceland, working with clients locally and internationally. I take on short, focused engagements or ongoing support.
